Introduction

RWAMdb offers a comprehensive repository of information on wetland autotrophic microorganisms, encompassing the entire workflow from field sample collection to gene annotation. With its user-friendly web interface, RWAMdb provides diverse browsing and search functionalities, ensuring precise and efficient access to relevant data. Additionally, the database integrates a suite of visualization and analytical tools, including BLAST, pathway completeness visualization via heatmaps, and autotrophic pathway mapping (APM).
